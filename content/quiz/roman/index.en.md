The _Capitolium_, dedicated to the Capitoline Triad of Jupiter, Juno and Minerva, was built in 73 AD under Emperor Vespasian and is part of the UNESCO World Heritage Site _Brixia_. The temple has three cellas and still impresses with its preserved floor mosaics and fresco fragments. Together with the adjacent theatre and the famous _Vittoria Alata_, it ranks among the most significant Roman remains in Italy.
