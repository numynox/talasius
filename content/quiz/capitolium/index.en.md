**Answer:** Brescia

This impressive Roman Capitolium is located in Brescia along the Via dei Musei. Built in 73 AD, it was the main temple of the ancient city of _Brixia_. Today it is part of the UNESCO World Heritage Site and is one of the best-preserved Roman temples in Northern Italy.
