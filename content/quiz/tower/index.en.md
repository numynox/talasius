The _Torre Mirabella_ is the oldest tower of the castle complex – its foundations go back to a Roman watchtower from the 1st century BC that secured the strategically important Cidneo hill. In the Middle Ages it was incorporated into the growing fortification and served as a lookout and signal tower.
