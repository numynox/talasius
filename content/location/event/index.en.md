Our wedding celebration will take place at the beautiful "El dos del Bec," an exclusive venue picturesquely situated on the hills above Lake Iseo. From here, you can enjoy a breathtaking panoramic view over the entire lake and the surrounding landscape, which is especially unforgettable at sunset. The idyllic location promises an intimate and festive atmosphere, perfect for this special occasion.

"El dos del Bec" is renowned for its elegant architecture, which harmoniously blends with the natural surroundings, as well as for its exquisite cuisine that combines local specialties with a modern flair. The expansive gardens and terraces offer numerous opportunities for lingering and celebrating outdoors. We are confident that this unique setting will provide the perfect backdrop for our wedding celebration, and we look forward to sharing this magical place with you.
