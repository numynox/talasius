Explore the historic city of Brescia with its Roman ruins, medieval castles, and impressive museums. Take a stroll through the charming alleys and enjoy the local cuisine.

You should definitely visit the UNESCO World Heritage site of the Roman Forum and Capitolium, testaments to the city's Roman past. Also, exploring the impressive Castello di Brescia is a mustâ€”it's a fortress perched on a hill offering panoramic views of the city and the surrounding landscape. Inside the castle, you'll find museums that shed light on the region's history and art.

For art lovers, the Pinacoteca Tosio Martinengo boasts a rich collection of paintings, including works by Raphael and other Italian masters. Don't miss the Brescia Cathedral, which encompasses both an old and a new cathedral, both architecturally stunning. Conclude your visit with an aperitivo in Piazza della Loggia, one of Italy's most beautiful squares, and immerse yourself in the city's vibrant atmosphere.
